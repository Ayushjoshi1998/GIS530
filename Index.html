A Beginners Guide to 'Kittens'


If you are a first time cat owner and need some help, you've come to the right place. Here are a few 'dos' and 'donts' that you may not be aware of. It is important to keep these in mind as cats are very different from humans and require us as petowner to respect these differences.

Dos

- Give them attention at least every 4 hours

- Open your windows/blinds

- Feed them in a schedule

- Keep them mentally stimulated

- Take them for regualr checkups

Donts

- Feed them grapes, chocolates, garlic, onions

- Feed them milk that isn't lactoce free

- Leave them alone for longer than 24hrs

- Leave litterbox dirty

- Leave fragile items in high places
